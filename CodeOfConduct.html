Code of Conduct - Atlas V

This Code of Conduct applies to all interactions within spaces controlled or organized by Atlas V.

Commitment to Respect

I am committed to creating a safe, welcoming, and inclusive environment for everyone. Harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior of any kind, whether directed at me or others, is not tolerated under any circumstances.

Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces, events, and interactions under my control, whether online or in person.

Enforcement

Anyone who violates this Code of Conduct will be promptly removed from these spaces without exception. Appropriate action will be taken to prevent further violations.

Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, you are encouraged to report it directly to me so that it can be addressed swiftly and fairly.
